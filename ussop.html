“I may be a liar, but I’ll become a brave warrior of the sea!”

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates, known for his sharpshooting skills, clever inventions, and resourcefulness in battle. Though he often appears cowardly and anxious, Usopp possesses a brave heart and a deep sense of loyalty to his friends. He uses a slingshot called Kabuto and an array of trick weapons and gadgets he invents himself to fight enemies from a distance. Usopp is also a talented storyteller, often exaggerating tales of his adventures, which boosts the morale of his crewmates. Over time, he grows from a timid liar into a courageous warrior, earning the respect of the crew and becoming an invaluable member of the Straw Hat Pirates.
