“I want to live and uncover the true history of the world!”

Nico Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates, calm, mysterious, and dangerously intelligent. From the age of eight, she was branded a criminal simply for wanting to uncover the forbidden history of the Void Century. She possesses the Hana Hana no Mi (Flower-Flower Fruit), granting her the ability to sprout limbs anywhere — turning elegance into lethal precision. Beneath her composed exterior lies a woman who has endured betrayal, loneliness, and loss — yet still chooses hope. Her mind is a weapon sharper than any blade, and her loyalty to her crew runs deeper than the seas they sail. When she says “I want to live,” it’s not just a plea — it’s a declaration of strength, survival, and rebirth.
