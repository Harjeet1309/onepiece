“I’ll protect my friends and fight for the seas with honor!”

Jinbe , the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates, is a symbol of honor, loyalty, and wisdom. A proud fish-man and former member of the Sun Pirates, he has spent his life fighting against the injustice and discrimination faced by his kind. With unmatched mastery over Fish-Man Karate and the ability to command the very currents of the sea, Jinbe is a warrior who blends strength with serenity. Once a Warlord of the Sea (Shichibukai), Jinbe turned his back on the government to stand by his principles — proving his loyalty time and again, from aiding Luffy at Marineford to defying Big Mom herself. His calm presence and deep respect for friendship make him the moral anchor of the crew. When Jinbe joined the Straw Hats, it wasn’t just another addition — it was the sea itself pledging allegiance to Luffy. As Jinbe himself said with quiet pride: “I owe my life to that man. If I am to die, it will be as a member of the Straw Hat Pirates.”
