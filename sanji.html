“I’ll find the All Blue and protect my crew — no one gets in my way!”

Sanji , known as the “Black Leg” of the Straw Hat Pirates, is the crew’s master chef and one of its fiercest fighters. He combines his culinary genius with deadly martial arts, specializing in powerful leg-based kicks that can obliterate enemies in seconds. Calm, confident, and always suave, Sanji fights with style, often protecting his friends with unmatched courage. He is also a hopeless romantic, particularly towards women, but in battle, he’s merciless and strategic, using agility, speed, and precision to take down even the strongest foes. Sanji’s dream is to find the legendary All Blue, a sea where every fish from every part of the world can be found, proving he’s as ambitious as he is deadly.
